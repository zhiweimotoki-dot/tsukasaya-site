The Meaning of 筑沙

筑 (Build) — The act of construction. From ancient foundations to modern structures, 筑 represents bringing vision into physical form with precision and care.

沙 (Sand) — Granular, fluid, modern. The silicon in AI chips, the grains in concrete, the shifting landscape of style. 沙 embodies sophistication at the cutting edge.

Together, pronounced Tsukasa, 筑沙 stands for building the future with intelligence and style.
